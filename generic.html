Our Workflow

"Focus on being productive instead of busy."

From Concept to Completion

1.Initial Consultation and Project Scoping +
  • We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your project’s goals, vision, and budget.
  • Collaborate closely with your team to define the project scope and key objectives.
  • Identify specific requirements to ensure creative decisions align with your expectations.
  • Focus on building a solid foundation that guides the entire process.
  • Ensure clarity and precision from day one to prevent any misunderstandings later.
2.Detailed Man-Day Estimation+
  • Provide a detailed man-day estimation, breaking down each phase of production.
  • Outline timeframes for concept development, design, execution, and post-production.
  • Present transparent pricing so you’re fully informed of all costs upfront.
  • Plan each step meticulously to set realistic expectations for the project timeline.
  • Eliminate any ambiguity, ensuring you can proceed with confidence.
3.Execution with Continuous Collaboration +
  • Begin execution with a well-defined plan and maintain open communication.
  • Involve you at every key milestone to ensure your vision is being realized.
  • Incorporate your feedback continuously to adapt and refine the work.
  • Stay flexible throughout the process to accommodate any evolving requirements.
  • Deliver a product that matches your creative aspirations and exceeds expectations.
4.Final Delivery and Quality Assurance +
  • Conduct rigorous quality control, reviewing every detail to ensure perfection.
  • Ensure the final product meets the highest industry standards.
  • Align the finished VFX piece with your original vision and goals.
  • Deliver a polished and refined piece ready to elevate your project’s visual impact.
  • Strive for excellence, ensuring the VFX enhances storytelling and captivates audiences.
