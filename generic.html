Our Workflow

"Focus on being productive instead of busy."

From Concept to Completion

1.Initial Consultation and Project Scoping

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your project’s goals, vision, and budget. Our team will work with you to define the scope and requirements.

2. Detailed Man-Day Estimation and Planning

After understanding your needs, we provide a detailed breakdown of the man-days required for each phase, along with transparent pricing. This ensures there are no surprises along the way.

3. Execution with Continuous Collaboration

Our team starts the work, keeping you updated throughout the process. We encourage ongoing collaboration to ensure your feedback is integrated and your expectations are met.

4. Final Delivery and Quality Assurance

We deliver the completed VFX work, ready to enhance your project’s visual impact. Our rigorous quality control process ensures that every frame meets our high standards and your vision.
