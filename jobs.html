Job Openings

"We’re hiring! Be part of something bigger and grow with us."

Whether you’re an experienced VFX artist or just starting your journey, we offer a range of positions across various departments:

• Paint & Roto Artists
Join our world-class team in delivering seamless visual effects by preparing and enhancing footage for post-production.
• Compositors
Help create the final, polished shots that will leave audiences amazed by blending live-action with CGI elements.
• Matchmove Artists
Work closely with the VFX and animation teams to ensure the correct tracking and match-moving of shots in dynamic scenes.
• 3D Artists
Model, texture, and animate characters, environments, and visual elements that bring our clients' stories to life.
• FX Artists
Simulate and create stunning effects such as fire, water, destruction, and magical phenomena for feature films, TV series, and commercials.
• Production Coordinators
Manage workflows, deadlines, and communication across departments to ensure seamless production from concept to delivery.
• VFX QC (Quality Control)
Ensure the highest standards of quality by reviewing and evaluating VFX shots, making sure they meet project specifications before final delivery.
• Team Leads
Lead, mentor, and manage VFX teams across various departments. Ensure smooth communication, technical direction, and project coordination to meet production deadlines and maintain quality standards.
• Line Producers
Oversee the day-to-day running of VFX projects, managing budgets, resources, and schedules. You’ll liaise between the production and creative teams, ensuring that projects are delivered on time and on budget.
